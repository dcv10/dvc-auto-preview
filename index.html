About DVC Auto

An Independent Workshop Built on Two Decades of Repairs

DVC Auto is an independent, RMI-registered repair workshop in Isando, Kempton Park. For more than 20 years we've kept Gauteng moving — from family cars and bakkies to trucks, forklifts and earthmoving equipment.

What started with everyday car repairs has grown into one of the few workshops on the East Rand that handles almost anything on wheels or tracks. Over the years we've built the experience, tooling and supplier relationships to take on jobs most general workshops turn away — including specialist earthmoving and forklift work.

Being RMI-registered means we're held to the standards of the Retail Motor Industry Organisation, so the workmanship and the way we treat customers are accountable to an independent body. Every job starts with a free, written estimate, and we'll never start work you haven't approved. That same approach is why fleet operators and local businesses trust us with their vehicles and equipment year after year.

Whether it's a single service, an insurance repair, or keeping a commercial fleet on the road with minimal downtime, you deal with a hands-on team that does the work itself — right here in Kempton Park.
